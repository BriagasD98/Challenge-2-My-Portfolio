About Me

My name is David Briagas and I'm 22 years old. I was born and raised in Houston,Texas. My hobbies are photography, auto mechanics and spending money on rusty 90's cars. I own two 90s Miatas - One which i daily drive and the other a track car project. Most people know me as "the guy with the Miata".

I graduated from Cinco Ranch High School in 2016. Like many high school graduates, i was lost on what to do next. I studied mechanical engineering at Lonestar College for two years but never finished because i lost interest in that field. I was always somewhat interested in coding since my freshman year in high school but didn't know where to start. I opted to finally learn coding when one of my friends expressed how learning to code changed his life. I wanted a career that i could enjoy, not just "a living".
